The following information provides the basis for the review and evaluation of the advice and sign posting services of the staff, employed by Derby Bosnia-Herzegovina Community Association.

The Derby Bosnia-Herzegovina Community Association will consider only "relevant complaints":

the competence or fitness of a adviser who provide advice or services

the competence or fitness of person working under the supervision of the adviser employed by Bosnia-Herzegovina Community Association

alleged breaches of the rules of the relevant regulatory body

overall performance off the employed staff

overall satisfaction of the Bosnian "One Stop Shop" Centre
