Our Bosnia-Herzegovina Community Association (One Stop Shop) and "The Refugees Support Refugees Project" in Bosnia & Herzegovina Centre, which is located at 99 Curzon Street in Derby, is open Monday to Friday between the hours of 9am and 5pm, and on Saturdays from 12pm to 4pm.
