Outreach Worker


The Project partners:

Derby Bosnia Herzegovina Community Association (DBHCA) and
Derby Refugee Forum (DRF)

Outreach Worker – part time (Post No: 005/17)

Scale 3, SCP 14 £18,734 pro rata, up to 31-08-2019 Term Contract (18 hrs).

DBHCA and DRF Project Partners are seeking to recruit an experienced and talented individual with a caring and highly professional attitude to vulnerable Bosnian and Former Yugoslavia refugees and Roma people who are amongst the most marginalised in our society. This will involve working with people who have physical, mental health problems and low self esteem.

Expect to carry out some paperwork, monitoring and recording, support and engaging clients in activities. Being a bilingual in one of our community languages could be an advantage.

Closing date for applications: 25th August 2017 at 17.00.

Interviews will be held before 15th of September 2017.

Ask for application pack via: e-mail: bihcommderby@hotmail.com or download application form from website on www.bihcommunityderby.co.uk. Alternatively, you can send an A4 size SAE with 1.86 p stamp to: Mr. F. Kevric, Bosnia & Herzegovina Centre, 99 Curzon Street, Derby, DE1 1LN, asking for application pack.

For more information please telephone: 01332294436, 07971057969.

Funded by: Big Lottery Fund's Reaching Communities Programme.
